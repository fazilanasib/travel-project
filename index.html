Why Dubai?

Dubai is the capital of United Arab Emirates and one of the wealthiest cities in the world. Dubai is full of adventures. With many attractions such as beaches, flower gardens, tall buildings, and various business opportunities, there is so much to love Dubai for. To learn more, click here!

My favorite attractions in Dubai...

Global Village

"A world where more than 90 cultures come together to celebrate wonder. A world where new and exciting can be found around every corner, where colours, flavours and experiences blend into one magnificent cultural melting pot." To learn more, click here.

Address:

3895+466 القرية السورية - Sheikh Mohamed Bin Zayed Road and Emirates Road - Exit 37 - E311 - Dubai

What I like about it:

I loved experiencing the whole world in one location. The way every country is represented with it's foods, architecture, clothing, etc. It is absolutely incredible. It is, definitely, one of my favorite places to be in Dubai.

Dubai Fountain Show

"A captivating water, music and light spectacle in Downtown Dubai, The Dubai Fountain is the world’s tallest performing fountain. At over 900 ft in length – equivalent to over two football pitches – The Dubai Fountain is situated on the 30-acre Burj Lake and performs to a selection of different melodies." To learn more, click here.

Address:

Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Blvd - Downtown Dubai - Dubai - United Arab Emirates

What I like about it:

Once you are at the Dubai Mall, there is a dancing fountain show every hour. It is incredible. I have never seen anything like it. The water moves with the beat of the music creating beautiful rhythym. It is a once in a lifetime must watch.

Dubai Miracle Garden

Each season, as the weather begins to cool at the onset of winter, the gates reopen at Dubai Miracle Garden. With more than 150 million flowers in full bloom, this 72,000-sq-m paradise has flowered into one of the region’s most picturesque, sweet-scented destinations since it first opened nearly nine years ago – fittingly, on Valentine’s Day." To learn more, click here.

Address:

Street 3 - Al Barsha - Al Barsha South - Dubai - United Arab Emirates

What I like about it:

Like the name, the miracle garden is truly miraculous. Everything is made of flowers. The way the garden is well kept and maintained is also mind blowing. It's definitely a place to visit.
